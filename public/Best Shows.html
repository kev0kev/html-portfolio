The Best Shows of All Time

My top 3 shows

  1. Mr. Robot

    2. Elliot Alderson, E-Corp, F-Society, need I say more. This is one of the best psychodramas out there. Inspirational for a budding techie!

  2. Suits

    3. Aah, suits. The story of Mike, a fake lawyer (though much better than most of those qualified) who ends up living an enviable life of a hotshot Harvard graduate working for a top tier law firm. I watched this a long time ago and it still never ages.

  3. Billions

    4. Money, politics, power. Hedge funds and brinkmanship between Bobby Axelrod and Chuck Rhoades. This one was brilliant. Although in all honesty, it lost some juice towards the later seasons but still thrilling to watch!
